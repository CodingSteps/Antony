Hypsilophodon

Hypsilophodon

Hypsilophodon means "High-Crested Tooth". It lived in the Early Cretaceous Period. It's fossils were found in England. It measured 5.9 ft in length, 2 ft in height and weighed 44 lbs. It ate low-growing vegetation. It was a biped and a quadruped. Its fore legs were adapted to climb on rocks or trees. They lived in large herds. Neovenator and Eotyrannus were its common predators.

T-REX (Tyrannosaurus Rex)

T - REX (Tyrannosaurus Rex)

Tyrannosaurus Rex means "Tyrant Lizard King". It lived in the Late Cretaceous Period. Its fossils were found in USA and Mongolia. It measured 40 ft in length, 13 ft in height and weighed 7 to 9 tons. It was the King of the Dinosaurs. It was the most dangerous dinosaurs of its time. It ate Triceratops, Edmontosaurus, Corythosaurus and Santanaraptor. It was also a scavenger and cannibal. It had a bite force of 12,000 psi! It had an excellent sense of smell, sight and hearing. Althogh it had tiny arms, they were used for slashing its prey.

Argentinosaurus

Argentinosaurus

Argentinosaurus means "Lizard from Argentina". It lived in the Late Cretaceous Period. As the name implies, its fossils were found in Argentina. It was the largest of all land-dwellers. It measured 115 ft in length, 70 ft in height and weighed an astonishing 90 to 110 tons! Although it had an extremely huge size, predators like Giganotosaurus and Mapusaurus can still bring it down.
